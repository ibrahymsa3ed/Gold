## Introduction

InstaGold (“the app”) helps you track gold prices and manage personal gold-related records on your device. This policy describes what information is processed and how.

## Information we collect and why

### Account and authentication

- If you sign in with **Google** or **email/password** via **Firebase Authentication**, Google/Firebase process account identifiers needed to sign you in. We use this to identify your session in the app.

### Data you enter in the app

- **Asset and savings information** you choose to enter (for example weights, karat, purchase notes) is stored **locally on your device** unless you use features that send data elsewhere as described below.

### Price information

- The app may retrieve **public gold price information** from third-party websites or public feeds (for example market or channel summaries) to display current prices. This typically does not include personal data.

### Notifications

- If you enable alerts, the app may schedule **local notifications** on your device. We do not need to send your name or gold holdings to a server for basic local reminders.

### Backups

- If you use **export/backup**, data may be written to a **file you control** (for example a zip on your device or a cloud folder you choose). If you connect **Google Drive** or similar, their terms and privacy policy also apply to files you upload.

### Optional advertising

- **If the app shows ads** (for example through Google AdMob), the ad provider may use identifiers such as the **Advertising ID** and technical data to serve and measure ads, subject to Google’s policies and your device settings. You can limit ad personalization in your **Google/Android settings** where available.

### Invoices or attachments

- If you attach **files** (for example invoice images) to an asset, those files stay on your device unless you include them in a backup you export.

## Legal bases (where applicable)

- **Performance of the service** — operating the app and features you request.  
- **Legitimate interests** — keeping the app secure and improving stability, where allowed by law.  
- **Consent** — where required (for example optional notifications or ads), you can withdraw consent in device or app settings.

## Sharing

- We do not sell your personal information.  
- **Service providers** (for example Google/Firebase for authentication, or ad networks if enabled) process data under their terms to provide those features.  
- We may disclose information if **required by law**.

## Retention

- Data stored **only on your device** remains until you delete the app or erase data in the app.  
- **Firebase Authentication** data is retained according to Firebase/Google settings for your project.

## Security

- We use reasonable measures appropriate to the app (for example secure transport for sign-in where applicable). No method is 100% secure.

## Children

- The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are not allowed to agree to this policy in your region.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to **access, correct, delete, or export** personal data, or to **object** to certain processing. Contact `[YOUR_EMAIL]` for requests. You can also manage some data in **Google Account** settings and **Firebase**-linked accounts.

## Changes

- We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.
