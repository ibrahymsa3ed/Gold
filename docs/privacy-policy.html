Privacy Policy — InstaGold

App name: InstaGold  |  Package: com.ibrahym.goldfamily  |  Last updated: May 5, 2026

Introduction

InstaGold ("the app") helps you track gold prices and manage personal gold-related records on your device. This policy describes what information is processed and how.

Information we collect and why

Account and authentication

If you sign in with Google or email/password via Firebase Authentication, Google/Firebase process account identifiers needed to sign you in. We use this to identify your session in the app.

Data you enter in the app

Asset and savings information you choose to enter (for example weights, karat, purchase notes) is stored locally on your device unless you use features that send data elsewhere as described below.

Price information

The app retrieves public gold price information from third-party public sources to display current prices. This does not include personal data.

Push notifications and price alerts

If you enable price alerts or notifications, a device token is registered with our backend (hosted on Railway) to deliver push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging. No gold holdings or personal financial data is sent to the server — only a device identifier, locale, and notification preferences.

Backups

If you use export/backup, data may be written to a file you control (for example a file on your device or a cloud folder you choose). If you connect Google Drive or similar, their terms and privacy policy also apply to files you upload.

Advertising

The app currently does not show ads. If advertising is enabled in a future version, we will update this policy accordingly.

Attachments

If you attach files (for example invoice images) to an asset, those files stay on your device unless you include them in a backup you export.

Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Performance of the service — operating the app and features you request.
  • Legitimate interests — keeping the app secure and improving stability.
  • Consent — where required (for example optional notifications), you can withdraw consent in device or app settings.

Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Service providers (Google/Firebase for authentication and push notifications) process data under their terms to provide those features.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

Retention

  • Data stored only on your device remains until you delete the app or erase data in the app.
  • Firebase Authentication data is retained according to Firebase/Google settings for your project.
  • Device tokens registered for push notifications are removed automatically when you unregister or the token becomes invalid.

Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to the app (for example secure HTTPS transport). No method is 100% secure.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are not permitted to agree to this policy in your region.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data, or to object to certain processing. Contact us at the email below for requests. You can also manage some data in your Google Account settings.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.
