## Introduction

InstaGold ("the app") helps you track gold prices and manage personal gold-related records on your device. This policy describes what information is processed and how.

## Information we collect and why

### Account and authentication

If you sign in with **Google** or **email/password** via **Firebase Authentication**, Google/Firebase process account identifiers needed to sign you in. We use this to identify your session in the app.

### Data you enter in the app

**Asset and savings information** you choose to enter (for example weights, karat, purchase notes) is stored **locally on your device** unless you use features that send data elsewhere as described below.

### Price information

The app retrieves **public gold price information** from third-party websites and public feeds to display current prices. This does not include personal data.

### Notifications

If you enable alerts, the app schedules **local notifications** on your device. No personal data is sent to a server for this feature.

### Backups

If you use **export/backup**, data is written to a **file you control** (for example a zip on your device or a cloud folder you choose). If you connect **Google Drive** or similar services, their terms and privacy policy also apply to files you upload.

### Invoices or attachments

If you attach **files** (for example invoice images) to an asset, those files stay on your device unless you include them in a backup you export.

## Sharing

- We do not sell your personal information.
- **Service providers** (for example Google/Firebase for authentication) process data under their own terms to provide those features.
- We may disclose information if **required by law**.

## Retention

- Data stored **only on your device** remains until you delete the app or erase data within the app.
- **Firebase Authentication** data is retained according to Firebase/Google settings for the project.

## Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to the app (for example secure transport for sign-in). No method is 100% secure.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are not allowed to agree to this policy in your region.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to **access, correct, delete, or export** personal data, or to **object** to certain processing. Contact ibrahym.saaeed@thepetlabco.com for requests. You can also manage some data in **Google Account** settings and **Firebase**-linked accounts.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.
