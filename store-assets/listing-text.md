## English

### Short description (max 80 chars)

Track live gold prices in Egypt and manage your family's gold assets.

### Full description (max 4000 chars)

InstaGold is a powerful gold tracking app designed for the Egyptian market. Stay updated with live 21K, 24K, 18K, 14K karat prices, gold pound, and global ounce rates — all in one place.

Key features:

- Live gold prices: Auto-refreshed from trusted local sources with buy and sell rates for every karat.
- Jeweler's Dollar Gap: See the real-time gap between local and international gold prices at a glance.
- My Gold portfolio: Record rings, necklaces, bracelets, coins, and ingots with purchase price, weight, date, and optional invoice attachment. Track profit or loss on each item.
- Savings goals: Set gold savings targets and track your progress toward them.
- Family members: Manage gold assets for multiple family members under one account.
- Zakat calculator: Automatically converts mixed karats to 24K equivalent and checks the 85g Nisab threshold.
- Backup and restore: Export your data as a portable zip file and restore it on any device.
- Bilingual: Full Arabic and English support — switch anytime in settings.
- Dark and light themes: Choose the look that suits you.
- Price notifications: Get periodic alerts so you never miss a price move.

InstaGold works entirely on your device — no server subscription required. Sign in with Google, add your family members, and start tracking your gold today.
